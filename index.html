Sri BalaKashi Mahal, Coimbatore

Sri BalaKashi Mahal is located in Thekkalur of Coimbatore District. Sri Balakashi Mahal is newly built, with air conditioned halls and modern facilities. It is ideal for conducting family functions and other events.


Bus facility available from all parts of the city. Rent includes vessels, tables, chairs and 2 A/C Halls and 2 air-conditioned rooms.
